Saturn

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and the second-largest in the Solar System. Known for its stunning ring system, Saturn is a gas giant primarily composed of hydrogen and helium. Its atmosphere is thick with clouds of ammonia and methane, and it has at least 82 moons, with Titan being the largest.
